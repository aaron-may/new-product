How Did Anlage Come to be?

The design for the stylish, minimalist anlage came about when our engineers could not find rope that was aesthetic and versatile while still being high-class and durable. Our team worked for months to create anlage in our Brooklyn factory, working with a variety of materials to balance cost, strength, texture, and aesthetics. Finally, we settled on a nylon and polypropylene blend, expertly woven together and distributed so that anyone can enjoy the best material without sacrificing elegance or their wallet.
